**To:** Debbie McGrath, CEO

**Subject:** Engagement Decision for Asset Divestiture

You’re selling three assets: the hr.com domain ($15-35M), the hr.com media business (~$7-10M), and the MyPeople.ai platform ($7M revenue). This isn’t a pricing decision. It’s a decision about how you want us to run the market.

Do you want a standard process, a shared-risk model, or a fully-aligned, high-conviction sprint? Our job is to execute your chosen playbook with maximum aggression and transparency.

Here are the three ways we can engage.

---

### OPTION A — STANDARD PROCESS **(RECOMMENDED)**
We run three parallel tracks. Full court press on all assets simultaneously.
*   **Fee:** $15K/month advisory fee + 5% success fee on close.
*   **Term:** 90-day engagement.
*   **Tail:** 12 months. **Only applies to buyers we directly contacted AND who responded.**
*   **Best for:** Maximum control. Predictable economics. We’re incentivized to close, but you keep more upside. This is the efficient, operator-led path.

### OPTION B — PERFORMANCE ALIGNED
Same aggressive, parallel process. Fee structure shifts risk to us.
*   **Fee:** Reduced $8K/month + 4% success fee + performance bonus above an agreed floor price for each asset.
*   **Term:** 90-day engagement.
*   **Tail:** Same as Option A.
*   **Best for:** Shared risk, shared upside. You get downside protection on fees; we earn more only if we beat the baseline.

### OPTION C — FULL CONVICTION
We go all in. You get our full resources with zero out-of-pocket cost.
*   **Fee:** **$0 monthly fee.** 6% on first $40M total proceeds, 20% on everything above $40M.
*   **Term:** 90-day engagement. **We require exclusivity.**
*   **Tail:** Same as Option A.
*   **Best for:** Maximum incentive alignment. We only win if you win big. Requires full trust in the process and our joint goal to smash $40M+.

---

### WHAT THE TAIL CLAUSE ACTUALLY MEANS
This is critical. No fine print.
*   The 12-month tail **only** applies to buyers **we directly contact AND get a response from** during the engagement.
*   We provide a named buyer list at the start. It’s updated weekly.
*   You will see every name, every contact attempt, every response. Full transparency.
*   **Example:** If we pitch Oracle and they ghost us, they are **not** on the tail list. The tail is narrow, fair, and based on actual, provable work.

---

### OUR RECOMMENDATION: OPTION A
This is the clearest, most controlled path. It aligns our efforts without over-complicating the outcome. You maintain flexibility, we stay aggressively incentivized to close all three assets, and the economics are straightforward.

We run the market hard. You call the play.
