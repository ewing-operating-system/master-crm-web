CEO Vision & Strategy

Nadella's thesis is that AI is a platform shift as big as cloud, and Microsoft intends to own every layer of the stack — from infrastructure (Azure AI) through applications (Copilot) to workflow automation (agents). The strategy is to embed AI into every role and business process, grounded in enterprise data, and monetize through consumption growth. HR is one of the largest enterprise functions by headcount and budget, making it a critical vertical for this vision.
