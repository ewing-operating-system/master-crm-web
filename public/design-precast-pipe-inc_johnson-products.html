The 75-Year Signal: A family business that has operated for 75 years is almost always at an inflection point. The founder generation is long gone; the second and third generations are aging into the same decision the Fore family already made when they looked at succession. The question is not whether succession is coming — it is who gets the call first. Multi-Location as Leverage: Johnson Products runs plants in NC, SC, and GA. Acquiring this is not just buying revenue — it is buying a market position that cannot be replicated at any reasonable price by a greenfield entry.
