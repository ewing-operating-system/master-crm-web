Buyer Profile

Harcros Chemicals is a manufacturer and distributor of industrial and specialty chemicals in the United States, founded in 1917. The company has over a century of experience in chemical products including surfactants, emulsifiers, custom blends, absorbents, acetic acids, acid inhibitors, and specialty chemicals serving agriculture, personal care, oil and gas, and water treatment markets. Harcros employs approximately 385 people.

Under President and CEO W. Michael Miller (appointed in 2024), Harcros is entering a new strategic chapter. Miller brings over 28 years of experience within the organization, starting as a sales representative in 1995 and advancing through District Manager (Memphis, 2012), Regional Manager (2015, overseeing Little Rock, Vicksburg, and Nashville), to leading the development of Harcros Rivergate in 2017, a division specializing in transloading and unique logistical solutions. Miller's demonstrated ability to identify expansion and diversification opportunities positions Harcros for strategic growth.

Harcros' interest in AquaScience would be driven by the natural synergy between chemical supply and water treatment operations. As a specialty chemicals manufacturer, Harcros produces many of the chemical inputs used in water treatment processes. Acquiring AquaScience would represent forward integration -- owning the treatment operation that consumes the chemicals Harcros manufactures. This vertical integration creates margin expansion opportunities and strengthens customer relationships.
