Strategic Fit

Louisiana Adjacency: Alfred Miller Precast operates in Louisiana — directly adjacent to Design Precast's existing footprint in Biloxi, MS and the Arnaudville, LA corridor. A Louisiana acquisition would deepen Design Precast's Gulf Coast presence and extend market reach without a long-distance integration challenge.

Family Construction Lineage: A family born from bricklaying into precast manufacturing brings deep trade craft and a quality culture that is difficult to build from scratch. This is not a financial roll-up — it's a company with generational pride in their product, which translates to retained workforce and customer loyalty post-acquisition.

Succession Dynamics: Multi-generational family businesses frequently reach an inflection point where the next generation has diverging interests or the founding generation is approaching exit. Design Precast, as a fellow family-owned operator, is the ideal conversation partner — not a fund, not a stranger, but a peer who understands what was built.

Platform Logic: Combining Alfred Miller's Louisiana presence with Design Precast's Mississippi operations creates a two-state Gulf Coast manufacturing base with shared procurement, combined DOT bidding power, and a unified brand story for future strategic conversations.

Call opener: "We're a precast manufacturer out of Biloxi — family-owned, like you all. We've been active in Louisiana and we know the Miller name in this industry. We'd like to have a conversation, not a pitch — just a talk between people in the same business about where things are headed."
