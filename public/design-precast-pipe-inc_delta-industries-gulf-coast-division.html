Strategic Fit

Same Market, Local Competitor Tuck-In: Delta Industries' Gulf Coast Division operates in the Gulfport–Biloxi corridor — the same market as Design Precast. Acquiring this division would consolidate the Mississippi Gulf Coast precast market, eliminating a direct local competitor and gaining their customer relationships, production capacity, and workforce.

Divestiture Opportunity: Divisions of larger companies are sometimes divested when they fall outside the parent's core focus, underperform within the portfolio, or when the parent needs capital. Design Precast is ideally positioned to be the acquirer of record — local operator, immediate operational fit, and no integration complexity.

Market Consolidation Value: Two competing precast plants in the same Gulf Coast corridor means price competition, shared customer relationships, and split bidding on the same contracts. Consolidation eliminates that friction and creates a single dominant regional supplier with better pricing power and more complete coverage.

Workforce and Equipment: An operating precast plant with trained workers and production equipment in the same region is worth significantly more to Design Precast than to any out-of-market buyer. Local operating knowledge, existing customer familiarity, and proximity to Design Precast's own plant mean integration costs would be minimal.

Approach Strategy

Call opener: "We're a precast manufacturer here on the Gulf Coast — we operate in the same markets as your division. We've been watching the industry consolidate, and we think there may be a conversation worth having about the Gulf Coast operation. We'd like to connect with whoever handles strategic decisions for that division."

1
Frame the approach around the parent company's strategic interests — a non-core Gulf Coast division may be more valuable to them as cash than as an operating unit competing in a market they don't dominate.
2
Design Precast is the logical buyer — local, operating in the same market, able to integrate immediately with no transition disruption to the division's customers or employees. No out-of-market buyer can match that.
3
Consolidating the Gulf Coast precast market is good for both the seller (clean exit at fair value) and the acquired division's employees (absorbed into a stronger local operator rather than cut or relocated).
