Strategic Fit

H2O Innovation is among the highest-fit strategic buyers in AquaScience's universe. As a pure-play water treatment company with three complementary segments — Water Technologies & Services, Specialty Products, and O&M — it benefits from an AquaScience acquisition across all three lines simultaneously.

The WTS segment can deploy H2O's proprietary treatment technologies through AquaScience's established Wyoming customer base. The Specialty Products segment gains a new route for chemical and consumable cross-sell. The O&M segment absorbs AquaScience's operational contracts into a scalable managed-services portfolio. Wyoming becomes a new geographic market with immediate installed-base revenue.

H2O Innovation's explicit buy-and-build strategy, public-market capital access, and cultural proximity as a mid-market water operator make integration straightforward compared to a large industrial conglomerate. Both companies speak the same operational language.

Approach Strategy

Call Opener: "H2O Innovation has been one of the most active acquirers in North American water treatment — we represent an owner-operated treatment company in Wyoming that fits your three-segment model cleanly. WTS, specialty chemicals cross-sell, and O&M contracts are all in play. Worth a conversation?"

1
Three-segment synergy from day one. AquaScience's operations feed all three of H2O Innovation's business lines — not just one. This is a rare acquisition that is immediately accretive to WTS revenue, chemical sales volume, and O&M contract count.
2
Wyoming is a white-space geography. H2O Innovation's North American footprint has limited Mountain West presence. AquaScience delivers established customer relationships, local regulatory knowledge, and operational infrastructure in a new market.
3
Compatible scale and culture. Unlike Xylem or Veolia, H2O Innovation is a mid-market operator — AquaScience won't be lost inside a conglomerate. The integration would preserve local service culture while adding the resources of a public water technology company.
