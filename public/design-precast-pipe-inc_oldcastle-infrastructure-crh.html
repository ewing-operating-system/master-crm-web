Buyer Narrative

Oldcastle Infrastructure is the precast concrete and infrastructure products division of CRH plc, a global building materials leader headquartered in Dublin with North American operations managed from Atlanta. CRH is a publicly traded company (NYSE: CRH) with annual revenues exceeding $35 billion, making it one of the largest building materials companies in the world. Oldcastle Infrastructure specifically manufactures precast concrete, pipes, stormwater management systems, and related infrastructure products across a network of plants throughout the United States and Canada.

CRH has built its dominance through an aggressive, disciplined acquisition strategy — completing hundreds of acquisitions over the past several decades, often targeting regional manufacturers with strong local relationships and established market positions. Their playbook is consistent: acquire well-run regional operators, integrate them into CRH's supply chain and distribution network, and use scale advantages in procurement, logistics, and engineering to improve margins. They are not financial engineers — they are operators who understand manufacturing, and they value founder-built businesses with real customer relationships and regional expertise.

For a seller evaluating CRH/Oldcastle as a potential acquirer, the key consideration is permanence and capability. This is not a private equity fund that will flip the business in five years. CRH acquires to hold, integrating operations into a platform that has been built over generations. They have the balance sheet to close quickly, the operational knowledge to run precast and pipe manufacturing at scale, and a track record of retaining key employees and honoring the legacies of the businesses they acquire.

  • Acquired multiple stormwater and drainage product companies to build out Oldcastle Infrastructure's product portfolio
  • Acquired Texas-based and Florida-based precast operations to consolidate the Gulf Coast and Southern corridor
  • CRH completed 28 acquisitions in 2023 alone across its building materials and infrastructure divisions

Why This Seller

Design Precast & Pipe operates a full-service precast concrete and pipe manufacturing facility in Gulfport, Mississippi — a Gulf Coast market where Oldcastle Infrastructure currently has limited or no manufacturing presence. For CRH, this is a textbook bolt-on acquisition: it fills a geographic gap in their Southeast network, adds capacity in a region experiencing accelerating infrastructure spending driven by federal highway bills and Gulf Coast resilience projects, and brings established relationships with Mississippi, Louisiana, and Alabama DOTs, municipalities, and contractors that would take years to build organically. The company's product lines — precast structures and pipe — align directly with Oldcastle's core business, meaning integration risk is low and operational synergies in procurement, engineering standards, and logistics are immediately available. At an estimated $15M in revenue, this is the exact size and profile of business CRH has acquired hundreds of times: a founder-built, regionally dominant manufacturer with real capabilities and real customers.
