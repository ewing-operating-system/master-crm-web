Your Company Story

HR.com is a 27-year-old profitable B2B digital media company — not a software business. Think of it as LinkedIn just for HR people. Content comes from the community.

The business includes four divisions: the media/community platform, HR West conference, Human Resource Research Institute, and a cert prep business. They also maintain a directory of 50,000 HR vendors.

Revenue has declined over the last 3 years due to investment focus on My People (spun-out platform tech). EBITDA is approximately 20%. Debbie knows virtually every potential buyer personally.
