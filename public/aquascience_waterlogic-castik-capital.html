Buyer Profile

Waterlogic, backed by Castik Capital, is a global provider of point-of-use water purification and dispensing solutions. The company was acquired by Castik Capital and subsequently merged with Culligan International, creating a global water treatment and purification powerhouse. Culligan International Company, owned by private equity investors including BDT & MSD Partners, operates as one of the world's largest water treatment companies.

The Waterlogic-Culligan combination created a comprehensive water solutions platform spanning residential, commercial, and industrial water treatment markets globally. Culligan's extensive dealer network and brand recognition, combined with Waterlogic's point-of-use purification technology, produces a water treatment company with unparalleled market reach and product breadth.

The strategic rationale for Waterlogic/Culligan acquiring AquaScience centers on geographic density building in the U.S. market. Culligan operates through a combination of company-owned and dealer operations, and regularly acquires independent water treatment operators to expand its network. AquaScience's Wyoming operations would fit naturally into Culligan's U.S. growth strategy, adding a local water treatment platform in a geography where Culligan may have limited presence.

Culligan's acquisition approach is highly efficient: the company has completed hundreds of acquisitions of independent water treatment dealers and operators over its history. The integration playbook is well-established, and the company provides acquired businesses with access to Culligan's brand, proprietary water treatment technology, supply chain advantages, and national account relationships while preserving local customer service relationships.

For AquaScience's owners, a Waterlogic/Culligan acquisition offers the stability of a global water treatment platform with established integration capabilities. The transaction process would benefit from Culligan's extensive M&A experience and standard acquisition terms, potentially streamlining the negotiation and closing process. AquaScience's employees and customers would gain access to Culligan's global resources while maintaining local service quality.
