## Centre Partners Launches TruTemp, a Field Services Platform, with Investment in Maynor Service Company

New York, September 16, 2025 – Centre Partners (“Centre”), a leading middle market private equity firm with offices in New York and Los Angeles, and Baldwin Creek Partners (“BCP”), an investment firm based in Connecticut, announced today the formation of TruTemp Holdings, LLC (“TruTemp” or the “Company”), through the acquisition of Maynor Service Company (“Maynor”). - Maynor establishes the foundation for the TruTemp platform, which will pursue a consolidation strategy within the HVAC, Plumbing and Electrical services (“Field Services”) sector in the Southeast.
