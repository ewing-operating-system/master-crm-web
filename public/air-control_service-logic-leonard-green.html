Strategic Fit

Service Logic is the largest privately held commercial HVAC platform in North America, having built 140+ locations through acquisitions since 2004 under Leonard Green and now Bain Capital and Mubadala. Bain closed the $3.1B take-private in December 2024, meaning fresh institutional capital is now actively deploying. Air Control sits in the DMV — one of the most government-dense HVAC markets in North America — which Service Logic does not yet have covered at scale.

Service Logic's stated headline is "mission-critical" commercial HVAC. The Northern Virginia/McLean corridor is precisely that: federal buildings, data centers, government contractors, and defense facilities. Air Control's existing relationships in this market represent years of institutional trust that cannot be organically replicated quickly. For a platform heading toward a Bain exit story, DMV density is a gap that needs to be filled.
