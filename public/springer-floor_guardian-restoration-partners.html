Strategic Fit

Guardian Restoration Partners is Alpine Investors' commercial restoration roll-up, launched April 2024 with three simultaneous acquisitions (DryLux, Dry Kings, Midwest Restoration). As of January 2026 they have closed 14+ deals at an approximate pace of one per month — with geographic reach across AZ, CA, WI, CO, and IL, plus active expansion into Boise and Seattle.

Commercial floor care is a natural bolt-on to water and fire damage restoration. Hard-surface floor stripping, refinishing, and restoration after a water event is already part of what restoration contractors must provide to return a commercial property to pre-loss condition. Springer Floor's trained technicians and commercial floor care equipment would allow Guardian to bring this work in-house rather than subcontracting it, improving margins and reducing job completion time.

Beyond the service overlap, Springer Floor would add a recurring commercial floor maintenance revenue stream — predictable B2B contract revenue that balances the project-based, insurance-driven revenue of restoration work. This is exactly the kind of cash flow diversification that Alpine Investors values in a platform build.

Approach Strategy

Call Opener: "You're acquiring restoration companies at one per month — we represent a commercial floor care company that handles the hard-surface side of every water and fire job you do. I'd like to have a quick conversation about whether there's a bolt-on acquisition that deepens your scope on every job site and adds recurring maintenance revenue."
1
Hard-surface floor restoration is already inside Guardian's scope of work — every water damage job requires it. Acquiring Springer Floor converts a subcontracted cost center into a captive margin driver across the entire platform.
2
Springer Floor's commercial maintenance contracts provide recurring monthly revenue — a fundamentally different cash flow profile than project-based restoration work, which gives Alpine a portfolio revenue quality argument to lenders and future acquirers.
3
Alpine is already acquiring one company per month — Guardian's integration infrastructure is built for speed. A commercial floor care acquisition is lower complexity than a full restoration operator and can be absorbed quickly.
