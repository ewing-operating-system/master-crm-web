## About Redwood Services

Redwood Services works with essential home service companies in the HVAC, plumbing, and electrical sectors. The company provides investments, coaching, and sharing of practices to partner companies, with a focus on the residential home services market, excluding new construction projects. It was founded in 2020 and is based in Memphis, Tennessee.
