Strategic Fit

Neighborly is the largest home services franchisor in the world with 28 brands and 5,500+ locations — and HVAC, through Aire Serv, is one of their core essential categories. The Northern Virginia corridor is a high-income, high-density residential market where Neighborly has franchise presence in adjacent trades but demonstrably weak HVAC coverage relative to the market's size and spend profile.

The competitive risk is real: Ally Services just picked off J Hood in Manassas, adding to their NoVA HVAC footprint. If Neighborly doesn't move on McLean, a competing platform — Aire Serv, Service Experts, or another national brand — will establish a stronger position in Fairfax County first. Air Control's existing customer base and brand recognition in McLean represent a faster path to market penetration than a greenfield franchise start.

Tim Sedgwick has been running Neighborly operations since 2020 and the platform has added 8+ brands in recent years — the integration infrastructure is built for this. A single-site HVAC operator in a proven market is a low-complexity bolt-on for a machine that already onboards franchise locations at national scale. KKR's backing ensures the capital to move quickly when the right asset surfaces.
