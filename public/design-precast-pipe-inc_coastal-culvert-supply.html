Strategic Fit

Eunice is 175 miles west of Biloxi — across the Louisiana border into Acadiana. This extends Design Precast's footprint westward along the Gulf Coast infrastructure corridor, opening the Lafayette and Baton Rouge markets that are currently outside Design Precast's immediate delivery radius. RCP and box culverts are Design Precast's core product categories; Coastal Culvert runs the same lineup. This is a clean operational merger — same equipment, same product specs, same customer type (DOT contractors and public works departments).

The Tricon license is a strategic asset. Tricon Precast licensees share product specs, pricing benchmarks, and often referral networks across the Gulf South. Acquiring a Tricon licensee brings that network relationship into the Design Precast fold — a benefit that extends beyond Coastal Culvert's local customer base.
