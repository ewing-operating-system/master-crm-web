Why This Seller

Design Precast & Pipe fills the most obvious geographic gap in NWPX's national infrastructure platform: the Gulf Coast. With manufacturing operations in Gulfport, Mississippi, Design Precast gives NWPX production capacity in a region experiencing significant infrastructure investment — from hurricane resilience projects to Gulf Coast port expansion and petrochemical facility buildouts. Pat Fore III has built a $15M revenue operation with an established customer base and production infrastructure that NWPX cannot replicate quickly through organic growth. Rather than spending years and capital building a greenfield Gulf Coast facility, NWPX gets an operating business with existing customer relationships, trained workforce, and permitted manufacturing capacity on day one. The precast and pipe product overlap is nearly perfect — this isn't a diversification play, it's a geographic platform extension of exactly what NWPX already does.
